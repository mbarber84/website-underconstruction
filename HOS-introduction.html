Harveys of Stretton Scenario Harveys of Stretton is a used car supermarket based in Stretton with customers from all over the UK. They sell used cars up to three years old and have an average stock of over 1500 cars on display at their extensive premises at any one time. They sell all makes and models. They use the latest industry leading technology to price check all their nearly new and used cars daily to ensure that customers get a fair deal on both part-exchange deals and on the cars they sell. Each car is given a pre-delivery inspection and full check-up in the onsite workshop and is sold with service history, specification, keys and documentation all recorded. They also include a 3-month warranty which can be extended to 12 months for an additional fee. Harveys of Stretton also offer finance deals on all the cars they sell and a delivery service for customers from all over the country.

Problems that could be solved using a business application from lesson discussion: • Efficient and accurate way to log cars in when they arrive on site, • After sales register/bookings, • Get customer ID and take accurate customer details (generate future sales), • Employ young people (apprentices) to improve efficiency, • Parking lot register, • Test drive bookings: log sales and keys, Problems that could be solved using a business application:
• An application to record full car details as soon as they arrive on site
• An application to book test drives & record key issue
